7.Cookies and analytics

This website uses cookies via Google Analytics and Vercel Analytics to collect general, non-identifying information about how visitors use the website, such as pages viewed and approximate geographic region. This information does not identify you personally. You can disable cookies at any time through your browser settings; doing so will not affect your ability to browse this website or submit an enquiry.

We take reasonable technical and organisational steps to protect personal information from misuse, loss, and unauthorised access, including the use of access-controlled database infrastructure. However, no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage, is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security of information you transmit to us.

9.Data retention

We retain personal information collected through this website for as long as necessary to respond to your enquiry and maintain an accurate client record, or as required by law, whichever is longer.
