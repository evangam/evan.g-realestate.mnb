Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This policy explains what happens to your information when you use this website. It's written in plain language rather than dense legal text — if anything is unclear, contact details are at the bottom of this page.

Who this policy covers

This website is the independently operated personal marketing site of Evan Gamkrelidze, a sales agent with Raine & Horne Mosman. It is not an official Raine & Horne corporate website, though listings and sales figures shown relate to work carried out through that agency.

What information is collected

When you submit the enquiry form on this site, the following is collected:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your phone number (if provided)
  • Any message you enter

The site also uses standard website analytics (Google Analytics and Vercel Analytics) which collect general, non-identifying usage data such as pages visited and approximate location — this is the same kind of data most websites collect and does not identify you personally.

How your information is used

Enquiry details are used solely to respond to your enquiry and to follow up regarding buying, selling, or appraisal requests. Your details are stored in a private customer database (via Supabase) and a personal contact management system, and are not sold, rented, or shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

Where your information is stored

Enquiry data is stored securely with Supabase, a third-party database provider, and may also trigger an email notification via EmailJS so enquiries can be responded to promptly. Reasonable steps are taken to keep this information secure, but as with any online system, no transmission over the internet can be guaranteed 100% secure.

Third-party services used by this site

  • Supabase — stores enquiry and listing data
  • EmailJS — sends email notifications when an enquiry is submitted
  • Google Analytics & Vercel Analytics — anonymous site usage statistics
  • Vercel — website hosting

Each of these providers has its own privacy practices governing the data they process on this site's behalf.

Cookies

This site uses standard analytics cookies (via Google Analytics) to understand how visitors use the site. You can disable cookies through your browser settings at any time; doing so won't affect your ability to browse listings or submit an enquiry.

Your rights

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal information held about you by contacting Evan directly using the details below.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in how the site operates. The "last updated" date above will reflect the most recent revision.

Contact

For any privacy questions or requests, contact Evan Gamkrelidze directly via the phone number or enquiry form on this site.
